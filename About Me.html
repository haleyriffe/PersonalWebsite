My name is Haley Riffe. I was born and raised in Carter County, KY in the town of Grayson. I was raised with 2 brothers and 3 sisters. Growing up with so many siblings as well as plenty of nieces and nephews has made me very family-oriented.

I graduated East Carter High School in 2017 and attended Morehead State University as a freshman in the fall of that year. I spent my 4 years at MSU making memories with my best friends, meeting my future husband Daniel, joining the Delta Zeta sorority, and learning how to be an elementary teacher.
In 2021, I graduated from MSU with my Bachelor's in Elementary Education. Soon after graduation, Daniel and I got engaged and began planning a wedding. Around the same time, I accepted a 2nd grade teaching position at Flemingsburg Elementary School. In that year, Daniel and I got married and celebrated our honeymoon over spring break. After a year there, I accepted a position as a 1st grade teacher at Owingsville Elementary School. During this school year I began my Masters degree to be a school librarian. And all that leads me to here! Learn more about my career experiences and accomplishments in the Career section.
